About Me

Hi! I'm Sadie Solomon and I run the shop EmmieBean here in sunny Oakland, California. I would love to work with you and illustrate your favorite photo! Take a look through the site to see how the process works, check out other customers' illustrations, and purchase your very own. I also love meeting my customers face-to-face and hearing the unique and funny stories behind your photographs. I sell at various handmade marketplaces around the country - and often give special fair discounts to my customers who purchase in person! Check out my Instagram for dates and locations.
